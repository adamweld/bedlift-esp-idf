Free Icon Resources

  1. Lucide Icons (Recommended)

  - URL: https://lucide.dev
  - Simple, consistent line icons
  - Easy to convert to pixel art for small displays
  - Open source (ISC license)

  2. Feather Icons

  - URL: https://feathericons.com
  - Minimalist, 24x24 pixel icons
  - Perfect for small displays
  - MIT licensed

  3. Material Symbols

  - URL: https://fonts.google.com/icons
  - Google's icon set
  - Various styles (outlined, filled, rounded)
  - Apache 2.0 license

  4. Bootstrap Icons

  - URL: https://icons.getbootstrap.com
  - Over 1800 icons
  - Clean, simple designs
  - MIT licensed

  5. Tabler Icons

  - URL: https://tabler-icons.io
  - 4000+ outlined icons
  - Highly consistent style
  - MIT licensed
